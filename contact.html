Contact

If you would like to contact me, you can reach me at:

help (at) port67 (dot) org

You’ll see emails written like this to help cut down on spam. There are programs that look all over the internet for email addresses to send random messages to. You can just use @ instead of (at), and . instead of (dot) to get my real email address. Surprisingly, this tiny adjustment is enough to stop most of them.

You can contact me about:

  • site issues
  • content issues
  • programming issues
  • just about anything you want

I will do my best to reply to you quickly! Please give me some time, but if a couple days pass without a response, feel free to resend your message.
